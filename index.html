SERVICES

We offer a comprehensive and integrated solution of audiovisual material for media and social networks. We have the resources, skills, and industry-specific experience to develop creative projects that are functional for the client.

Whether for the web or advertising campaigns, we create audiovisual pieces and photographs that boost their online presence and attract new consumers.

Furthermore, we specialize in creating content optimized for social media. From short videos in vertical format for stories, reels, and TikTok, to photos and videos in horizontal format for Instagram feed posts, we ensure that your content adapts perfectly to each platform, highlighting the uniqueness of each place and engaging with current trends.

ABOUT

In Cajú, two best friends, one filmmaker and the other musician, come together to reveal the essence of each space. With versatility, and a creatively vision, cultivated together throughout our lives, we approach every challenge with passion.
Like the grain that gives rise to the fruit, we identify the strengths of each establishment, and from that essential seed, the idea germinates. In a dedicated process, our creativity finds its most authentic expression, resulting in visually appealing pieces for those seeking a unique and memorable experience.
In Cajú, we not only create images, we weave stories that captivate the senses and nourish the soul. Each project is a masterpiece of our friendship and creativity, an expression of the beauty we find in every hotel experience.

At the age of 13, Camila Hayes inherited her mother's photo camera, marking the beginning of her profound love for photography and a constant pursuit of expressing herself through visual language, which led to her fascination with cinema. Years later, she graduated from the National University of Córdoba with a bachelor's degree in film and television and further specialized with a Master's in Cinematography from the Escola Superior de Cinema i Audiovisuals de Catalunya.
Mountaineering and rock climbing took her on adventures to remote places, where she discovered a profound curiosity for rural inhabitants and how their lives intertwine with the landscape. From this intrinsic relationship between humans and nature arise her aesthetic inclinations and her way of conceiving existence. Just as different weather atmospheres shape our moods, she seeks to create cinematic atmospheres that emotionally resonate with the viewer, as she considers art to be primarily an affective experience.

Julia Groppo grew up immersed in the world of music from an early age. Singer, guitarist, and drummer, she has explored a wide range of genres that have shaped her unique personal style. Her great passion for storytelling and boundless sonic expression, found in film music the perfect canvas to experiment with sound. She graduated from the Universidad Católica Argentina with a degree in Composition and Production of Cinematic Music, where she expanded her knowledge in piano, classical music, production, and sound design. Her diverse trajectory is now reflected in a remarkable versatility to tackle artistic projects. She skillfully blends the organic and the digital, silence and sound, abundance and simplicity, giving life to soundscapes that awaken consciousness.
